Comprehensive Legal Services

P. P. Shinde & Associates provides comprehensive legal representation before the High Court, District Courts, Consumer Courts, Revenue Authorities, Charity Commissioner, MahaRERA, and various statutory tribunals throughout Maharashtra.

RERA & Property Law

Expert handling of builder delays, carpet area verification, society redevelopment, and title search reports under MahaRERA.

Criminal Defense & Bail

High-priority bail applications, anticipatory bail, trial defense, and appeals across Sessions & High Courts.

Civil & Commercial Disputes

Injunctions, recovery suits, breach of contract, partnership disputes, and arbitration proceedings.

Consumer Court Litigation

Expert legal representation before District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC). We represent clients in matters involving defective products, deficient services, insurance claims, banking disputes, medical negligence, builder complaints, e-commerce disputes, and compensation claims.

Revenue & Land Matters

Professional representation before Revenue Authorities including Tahsildar, Sub-Divisional Officer (SDO), Collector, Divisional Commissioner, and Revenue Tribunal. Services include mutation entries, inheritance, land partition, boundary disputes, agricultural land matters, tenancy disputes, land records, property ownership disputes, and revenue appeals.

Charity Commissioner Matters

Legal assistance relating to Public Trust Registration, Change Reports, Trust Administration, Trustee Disputes, Scheme Matters, Property of Public Trusts, Religious and Charitable Institutions, and proceedings before the Office of the Charity Commissioner under the Maharashtra Public Trusts Act.

Family Court & Matrimonial Matters

Comprehensive support regarding Divorce, Child Custody, Maintenance claims, Domestic Violence proceedings, and Mutual Consent Divorce solutions handled with discretion and diligence.

Documentation & Registration

Drafting and registration execution of Sale Deeds, Gift Deeds, Leave & License templates, Partnership Deeds, structured Legal Notices, Affidavits, and tailored corporate Agreements.

Arbitration & Mediation

Efficient Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) methodologies helping corporate and individual clients secure fast out-of-court settlements and neutral settlement advisory options.
